I just got a new eBook reader, the Energy Sistem Pro. I was happy to discover that I could replace the screensaver and sleep pictures, so I made a bundle of some renders from crtrm and did just that. I adjusted them to display better on the greyscale screen, and I'm surprised at how well they turned out.

To use, download the zip, unzip the pictures, then copy them to your eBook, into the images or screensavers directory. To set one as the sleep image, view it fullscreen using the FileManager App, then tap the picture to make the menu appear, then tap the ebook icon on the menu.

If you place them all in the screensaver folder, then one will randomly be selected each time your EnergySis goes to sleep.

Enjoy!
